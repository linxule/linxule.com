# Sources

- **[The ASQ Blog](https://asqblog.com/)** - Behind the scenes of the Administrative Science Quarterly: A blog organized by students, for students.
  - Most posts are interviews with authors of recent and forthcoming ASQ articles. Two Ph.D. students ask five questions, and article authors answer.

- **[New Scholars](https://www.youtube.com/c/NewScholars/videos)** - Aims to nurture management scholarship which moves the world. Organizes discussions between established and emerging scholars.
  - [Collections – New Scholars](https://newscholars.network/collections/): Mats Alvesson and Davide Ravasi

- **[Strategic Management Society](https://www.youtube.com/c/StrategicManagementSociety)** - YouTube channel of the Strategic Management Society.

- **[Academy of Management](https://www.youtube.com/user/AcademyOfManagement/playlists)** - The Academy of Management (AOM) is the preeminent professional association for management and organization scholars.
