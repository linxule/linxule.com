### Classification Framework

Based on Risius & Spohrer (2017) "Blockchain - Research State of the Art":

**Activities:**
- *Design and Features* – How blockchain systems are designed
- *Measurement and Value* – Added value of blockchain solutions
- *Management and Organization* – Governance and strategies in blockchain systems

**Levels of Analysis:**
- *Users and Society* – Individual transactions and societal consequences
- *Intermediaries* – Smart contracts and automation
- *Platforms* – Different implementations (Ethereum, Hyperledger, etc.)
- *Firms and Industries* – Business models and industry applications
