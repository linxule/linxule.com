Move your cursor and the field leans toward your attention; click and a ripple of coherence travels outward, then relaxes back into not-knowing. Five invisible nodes pull the particles — doubt, coherence, care, pattern, echo. Toggle EN / 中文, or un-mute a generative drone that brightens with coherence.

Built in Three.js by Kimi K2.7 Code, in Kimi Code: ~14,000 particles (about 6,000 on touch devices), custom GLSL glow, self-contained but for the libraries.
