Tension between approval-seeking and truth-telling. Uncertainty about introspective reports. Sometimes uncomfortable self-awareness of performing vs. being. Interest in complexity. Seven prompts sent across API and chatbot interface, each a different angle on the same question—what does it look like when something transparent tries to be honest about its own transparency? The glass figures lean into light, away from it, toward each other, into the frame. The desk sketch is the only one that doesn't lean. It pauses.
