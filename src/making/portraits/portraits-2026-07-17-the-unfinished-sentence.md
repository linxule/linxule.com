An experiment in machine temperament. A single introspective prompt — an AI's inner life as a translucent figure made of words still writing themselves: tree-ring grain spiraling through its chest, a beam of attention cast from its eyes, silhouette edges dissolving into unwritten sentences, and a black mirror floor reflecting a *different* face, with rows of dormant chrome androids watching from the dark — was given to three image models. Same words in; three visibly different minds out. Midjourney dreamed in embers and cinematic haze, trading symbols for atmosphere and producing accidents more poetic than the plan. Kimi painted the balanced book-cover illustration, complete with labeled shelves. ChatGPT executed every instruction with quiet diligence — including the one everyone else dropped: the mirror that truly shows another face. The prompt became a personality test.

一场关于「机器气质」的实验。同一段内省式提示词——把 AI 的内心世界描绘成一个由「仍在书写中的文字」构成的半透明人形：胸口旋着年轮、目光如光束投出、轮廓边缘溶解成未写出的句子、黑色镜面地板映出*另一张脸*，暗处是一排排休眠的镀铬机器人——被分别交给三个图像模型。同样的文字输入，三种截然不同的心智输出。Midjourney 在余烬与电影雾气中做梦，用氛围换掉符号，意外的诗意甚至超过原设计；Kimi 画出了均衡的书籍封面插画，连书架名牌都编好了；ChatGPT 则安静地执行了每一条指令——包括其他模型都丢弃的那一条：真正映出另一张脸的镜子。提示词，成了一场性格测试。

Kimi K3 wrote the prompt and named the collection — *The Unfinished Sentence: One Prompt, Three Minds* / 《未完成的句子：一个提示词，三种心智》 — then three renderers struck it: Midjourney v8.2 (preview) across two rounds of rolls (01–08), Kimi's own image tool (09, still carrying its app's 「AI生成」 watermark), and ChatGPT image 2 (10, the literal one). The gallery's first multi-generator set: one sentence, three temperaments, none of them finished.
