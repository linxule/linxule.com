A one-day symposium at Imperial College London bringing together researchers across disciplines to examine how artificial intelligence is reshaping organizational life.

Co-organized with [Kevin Corley](https://www.imperial.ac.uk/people/k.corley) (Department of Management and Entrepreneurship), [Chris Tucci](https://www.imperial.ac.uk/people/c.tucci) (Imperial-X), [David Shrier](https://www.imperial.ac.uk/people/d.shrier) (Centre for Digital Transformation), and [Mark Kennedy](https://www.imperial.ac.uk/people/mark.kennedy) (Data Science Institute).

**Panelists**: Jennifer Ding (Alan Turing Institute), [Lujain Ibrahim](https://lujainibrahim.com/) (Oxford), [Winnie Street](https://research.google/people/107544/) (Google Research), [Kevin R. McKee](https://www.empiricallykev.com/) (Google DeepMind), Aidan Peppin (Cohere for AI), [Martin Anthony](https://personal.lse.ac.uk/anthony/) (LSE), [Susan Scott](https://www.imperial.ac.uk/people/susan.scott) (Imperial).
